Make It Last.

Project Agni, an initative by Enactus NSUT stands on a trinity of objectives. The project gives decommissioned firehoses a new battle to fight, one against mismanaged waste disposal.

Marginalized women are employed to craft our products. With their newfound financial independence, these women incarnate a phoenix rising from the ashes.

We offer an Environment-Conscious Alternative: trade leather with the firehose, replace pollution with sustainability.

Think Different.

Project Agni aims to upcycle decommissioned fire hoses and salvage their material. Our Team spends countless hours to brainstorm new and robust ways to convert these fire hoses into intricate & beautiful products.

Getting The Hoses.

Project Agni collaborates with fire departments in the New Delhi region to obtain decommissioned fire hoses. Agni (Fire) is also a metaphor for all the battles that our decomissioned fire-hoses have won, saving lives and properties.

Stitching To Freedom.

Project Agni impacts and improves lives by providing a livelihood and a status in the society to women who have faced violence.
This helps us form a close-knit community of women who help and empower each other.

HeartCrafted By Her.

Our Team invests significant time in understanding the Fire Hose Material to provide you with the best quality products. We, at Project Agni, use this super material and elevate it into handy products, heartcrafted by our beneficiaries.

Empowering Women.

Our customers are delivered products with an aided message of empowering the society and helping all of us to be a part of this prolific movement. The products are resilient, waterproof, and a sustainable alternative to leather. Their social value is their greatest merit.
