Enactus NSUT


TEAM AGNI

Project Agni is an initiative by the students of Enactus NSUT that aims to upcycle decommissioned fire hoses and empower women who’ve faced violence. With the incredible durability of the Fire Hoses, Our Team also solves the issue of Animal Abuse for Leather by providing a viable, reusable alternative for the same while adding value to the lives of the communities we employ.

The prospective product line caters to a diverse range of products ranging from Bags, Wallets, Belts, and various other accessories.



ENACTUS NSUT

Enactus NSUT is a community of passionate students committed to creating a new generation of socially responsible leaders.

We believe in having the perspective to see an opportunity and the talent to create value from that opportunity. We aim to impact as many lives as we can and create sustainable growth models for this world to be thriving with opportunities for all.

Enactus NSUT has taken various projects and events under itself since its inception. One such project is Project AGNI.





ABOUT ENACTUS

Enactus is a non-profit organisation that connects 37 countries, 1730+ university programs, 7200 student members, 550 corporate organisations, and individual partners that work for the exact cause of social entrepreneurship. They are working towards achieving the global goals of sustainable development.
